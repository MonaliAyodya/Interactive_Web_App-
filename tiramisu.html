Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a rich and creamy Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cheese, topped with cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 200g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • Dark chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks with sugar until thick and pale. Add mascarpone and mix until smooth.
  2. In another bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into mascarpone mixture.
  3. Dip ladyfingers briefly into coffee and layer in a dish.
  4. Spread half the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  5. Repeat layers and finish with mascarpone on top.
  6. Chill for at least 4 hours. Dust with cocoa powder and chocolate shavings before serving.

Tips

  • Use cold coffee and dip ladyfingers quickly to avoid sogginess.
  • Refrigerate overnight for the best flavor and texture.
