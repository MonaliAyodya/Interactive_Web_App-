Celery Soup

Description

This creamy and comforting Celery Soup is a light and healthy dish perfect as a starter or a cozy lunch. It’s simple to prepare and packed with fresh flavor from celery, herbs, and a hint of garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 6 cups chopped celery (about 10 stalks)
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil or butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté for 3–4 minutes until softened.
  2. Add chopped celery and diced potato. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the broth, add thyme, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20–25 minutes, until vegetables are tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth, or blend in batches in a blender.
  5. If using, stir in milk or cream for a richer texture. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.

Tips

  • For a vegan version, use olive oil and dairy-free milk or skip the cream altogether.
  • Add a squeeze of lemon juice before serving to enhance the flavor.
  • You can refrigerate leftovers for up to 4 days or freeze them for longer storage.
