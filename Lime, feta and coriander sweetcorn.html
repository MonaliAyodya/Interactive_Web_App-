Lime, Feta, and Coriander Sweetcorn

Description

This Lime, Feta, and Coriander Sweetcorn is a refreshing and zesty side dish that’s perfect for summer barbecues or a light meal. The sweetness of the corn pairs wonderfully with the tangy lime, creamy feta, and fresh coriander.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sweetcorn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh coriander (cilantro), chopped
  • Juice and zest of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. If using fresh corn, cook the sweetcorn in boiling water for 4-5 minutes until tender. If using frozen corn, thaw and drain it well.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked sweetcorn, crumbled feta cheese, and chopped fresh coriander.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, lime zest, olive oil, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the sweetcorn mixture and toss to combine until evenly coated.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with extra coriander or feta if desired.

Tips

  • For added flavor, grill the sweetcorn before using it in this recipe for a smoky taste.
  • If you prefer a bit of heat, add finely chopped chili or jalapeño to the mix.
  • This dish can be served cold or at room temperature, making it great for picnics or barbecues.
