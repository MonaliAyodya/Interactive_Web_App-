Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a comforting Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic, add ground beef, and cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and herbs, simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat.
  5. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 35–40 minutes or until bubbly and golden.

Tips

  • Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  • You can prepare it a day ahead and bake before serving.
