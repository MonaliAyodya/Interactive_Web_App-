Pumpkin Pudding Desserts

Description

This creamy pumpkin pudding is the perfect fall dessert. It's rich, spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg, and topped with whipped cream for a delicious treat everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) pure pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch pie dish or individual ramekins.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, heavy cream, milk, sugar, eggs, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, salt, and vanilla extract until smooth and well combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into the prepared dish or ramekins, then smooth the top with a spatula.
  4. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the pudding is set and a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Allow the pudding to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
  6. Before serving, top each portion with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon or nutmeg for extra flavor.

Tips

  • You can add crushed graham crackers or gingersnap cookies as a crunchy base for a layered dessert.
  • For an extra creamy texture, substitute the milk with half-and-half or full-fat coconut milk.
  • Try serving it in individual jars or glasses for a fun presentation.
