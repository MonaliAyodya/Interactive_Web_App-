Air-Fryer Scones

Description

These Air-Fryer Scones are quick, fluffy, and delicious — a great way to enjoy freshly baked scones without heating up your oven. Perfect with jam and cream for a classic treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, diced
  • 2/3 cup milk (plus extra for brushing)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional for a sweeter scone)
  • Jam and whipped cream, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix self-raising flour, salt, and sugar if using.
  2. Add the cold butter and rub it into the flour using your fingers until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Pour in the milk and gently mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and lightly knead. Pat to about 1-inch thickness.
  5. Cut out scones using a round cutter. Re-roll scraps to cut more.
  6. Preheat the air fryer to 320°F (160°C).
  7. Place scones in the air fryer basket on parchment paper, leaving space between each.
  8. Brush the tops with a little milk.
  9. Air-fry for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
  10. Serve warm with jam and whipped cream.

Tips

  • Don't overmix the dough; it keeps the scones light and fluffy.
  • Serve immediately or store in an airtight container for up to 2 days.
  • Try adding raisins, dried cranberries, or cheese for variation.
