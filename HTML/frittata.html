Frittata

Description

Frittata is an Italian egg-based dish similar to an omelette or crustless quiche, loaded with vegetables, cheese, or meats, and finished in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (like bell peppers, spinach, onions)
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or feta)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Sauté vegetables in an oven-safe skillet with oil or butter until tender.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the veggies and cook on the stovetop for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top and transfer skillet to the oven.
  6. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until eggs are fully set and golden on top.
  7. Let it cool slightly, slice, and serve warm or at room temperature.

Tips

  • Use any leftover vegetables or meats to customize your frittata.
  • Non-stick or cast iron pans work best for baking frittatas.
