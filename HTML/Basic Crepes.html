Basic Crepes

Description

These Basic Crepes are thin, soft, and perfect for sweet or savory fillings. They’re easy to make with simple pantry ingredients and make for a versatile breakfast, brunch, or dessert option.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and eggs.
  2. Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine.
  3. Add the salt and melted butter; beat until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of the batter into the skillet. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  6. Cook the crepe for about 1-2 minutes, until the bottom is light golden brown. Loosen with a spatula, flip and cook the other side.
  7. Repeat with remaining batter. Stack crepes on a plate and keep warm.

Tips

  • Let the batter rest for 30 minutes before cooking for best texture.
  • Fill with fruits, Nutella, cheese, or ham and eggs depending on sweet or savory cravings.
  • Crepes can be frozen—just layer with parchment and store in a zip-top bag.
