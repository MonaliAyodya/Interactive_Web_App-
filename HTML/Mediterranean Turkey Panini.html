Mediterranean Turkey Panini

Description

This Mediterranean Turkey Panini is a deliciously pressed sandwich layered with tender turkey, sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, feta cheese, and pesto — all packed between crispy grilled bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough or ciabatta bread
  • 2–3 slices of cooked turkey breast
  • 1 tablespoon basil pesto
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  • 2–3 sun-dried tomatoes (in oil), sliced
  • Handful of fresh spinach
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter (for grilling)

Instructions

  1. Spread pesto on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Layer turkey, sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, and feta cheese between the bread slices.
  3. Brush the outside of the bread with olive oil or butter.
  4. Place in a panini press or grill on a skillet over medium heat. Press down and cook until golden brown and heated through, about 3–5 minutes per side.
  5. Slice and serve warm.

Tips

  • Use whole grain bread for a healthier twist.
  • Swap turkey with grilled chicken or tofu for variation.
  • Add olives or roasted red peppers for extra Mediterranean flavor.
