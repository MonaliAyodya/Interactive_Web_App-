Smoothie Bowl

Description

This Smoothie Bowl is a vibrant and nutrient-packed way to start your day. Thick, fruity, and creamy—topped with fresh fruit, nuts, and seeds—it’s as beautiful as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt or dairy-free alternative
  • 1/4 cup milk of choice (almond, oat, dairy, etc.)
  • Toppings: sliced bananas, berries, granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes, honey

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen banana, frozen berries, yogurt, and milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and thick. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides or add a bit more milk for blending.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Top with your favorite toppings such as fresh fruit, granola, chia seeds, and a drizzle of honey.
  5. Serve immediately with a spoon and enjoy!

Tips

  • Use frozen fruit for a thicker consistency—like soft serve ice cream.
  • Customize toppings to your taste and dietary needs.
  • Add a scoop of protein powder or nut butter for an extra energy boost.
  • Try a tropical version with mango, pineapple, and coconut milk.
