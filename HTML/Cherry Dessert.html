Cherry Dessert

Description

This Cherry Dessert is a delicious and refreshing treat made with sweet, tart cherries and a buttery crust. It's perfect for summer or any occasion when you crave a fruity, creamy dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh or frozen cherries, pitted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9-inch square baking dish.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the cherries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and becomes bubbly. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, butter, powdered sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Gradually add the milk to the mixture, stirring until the dough comes together into a soft ball.
  5. Press the dough into the bottom of the prepared baking dish to form the crust. Bake for 15 minutes, or until the crust is lightly golden.
  6. Once the crust is done, remove it from the oven and spread the cherry mixture evenly over the crust.
  7. Return the dish to the oven and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the filling is bubbly and the top is golden brown.
  8. Let the dessert cool for about 15 minutes before serving. Serve warm or at room temperature, topped with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream for an extra treat.

Tips

  • If you don't have fresh cherries, you can use frozen cherries — just make sure to thaw and drain them before using.
  • For a slightly different flavor, try adding a pinch of almond extract to the filling.
  • Feel free to add a handful of chopped nuts, such as almonds or walnuts, to the crust for extra crunch.
