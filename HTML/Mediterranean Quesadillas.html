Mediterranean Quesadillas

Description

Mediterranean Quesadillas are a flavorful fusion of Mediterranean ingredients and Mexican style. Packed with feta, spinach, olives, and sun-dried tomatoes, they're cheesy, crispy, and utterly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup baby spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/3 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and brush lightly with olive oil.
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet. Sprinkle with half of the cheeses, spinach, tomatoes, and olives. Season lightly.
  3. Top with another tortilla and press gently. Cook for 2â€“3 minutes until golden, then flip and cook the other side.
  4. Remove and let rest for a minute before slicing into wedges.
  5. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Serve warm with tzatziki or hummus on the side.

Tips

  • Use whole wheat tortillas for a healthier version.
  • Add grilled chicken or chickpeas for extra protein.
  • Pairs perfectly with a Greek yogurt dip or tabbouleh.
