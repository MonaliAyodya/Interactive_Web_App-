Italian Wedding Soup

Description

Italian Wedding Soup is a hearty and flavorful dish featuring tender meatballs, green vegetables, and tiny pasta in a savory chicken broth. Despite its name, it’s not served at weddings—it refers to the perfect "marriage" of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef or pork (or a mix)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • 50g small pasta (like acini di pepe or orzo)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach or escarole

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix meat, egg, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Form into small meatballs.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pot and sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add meatballs gently and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add pasta and cook until al dente, then stir in greens and simmer until wilted.
  6. Season to taste and serve hot with extra Parmesan if desired.

Tips

  • You can bake or pan-fry meatballs before adding for extra flavor.
  • Substitute kale or chard if spinach is unavailable.
