Caprese Salad

Description

This classic Caprese Salad is a simple yet elegant dish made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil. It's a refreshing appetizer or side dish that captures the essence of Italian summer flavors.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange the tomato slices on a platter, alternating with the mozzarella slices.
  2. Place fresh basil leaves between the tomato and cheese slices.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil and balsamic vinegar (if using) over the salad.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.

Tips

  • For a different twist, try adding a drizzle of pesto or a few slices of avocado to the salad.
  • Use heirloom tomatoes for a more colorful and flavorful variation.
  • If you prefer, you can serve this salad chilled or at room temperature.
