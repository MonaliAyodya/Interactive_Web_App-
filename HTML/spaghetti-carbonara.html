Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Roman pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, savory, and super easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta (or bacon), diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Optionally add crushed garlic for extra flavor, then remove it.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with grated cheese and black pepper.
  4. Add the hot spaghetti to the pan with pancetta. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding a splash of reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Mix well and serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper on top.

Tips

  • Don’t add the egg mixture over direct heat — it will scramble.
  • Use freshly grated cheese for the best flavor.
