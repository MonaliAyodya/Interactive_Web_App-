Peanut Butter Banana Sandwich

Description

This Peanut Butter Banana Sandwich is a wholesome and satisfying classic. Creamy peanut butter paired with sweet banana slices makes for a delicious and energizing meal or snack thatâ€™s perfect any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole grain or white bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • 1 ripe banana, sliced
  • Optional: honey, cinnamon, or a sprinkle of chia seeds
  • Butter (if grilling the sandwich)

Instructions

  1. Spread peanut butter evenly on one or both slices of bread.
  2. Place banana slices over the peanut butter.
  3. Add a drizzle of honey or a sprinkle of cinnamon if desired.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread to make a sandwich.
  5. Optional: Butter the outside of the sandwich and grill on a skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Slice and serve warm or as-is.

Tips

  • For extra texture, try using crunchy peanut butter or adding chopped nuts.
  • Grilling the sandwich makes it warm, melty, and extra satisfying.
  • Use whole grain bread for a more nutritious option.
  • Great as a post-workout snack or a quick breakfast.
