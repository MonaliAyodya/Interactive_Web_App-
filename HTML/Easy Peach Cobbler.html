Easy Peach Cobbler

Description

This Easy Peach Cobbler is a simple and delicious dessert with a warm, fruity peach filling topped with a golden, biscuit-like crust. It’s perfect for any occasion, especially during peach season!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh or canned peaches, sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the peaches, granulated sugar, lemon juice, and cornstarch. Stir until well combined, then pour into the prepared baking dish.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon. Add the milk and melted butter, stirring until a thick batter forms.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the batter evenly over the peach mixture.
  5. Sprinkle the brown sugar over the batter.
  6. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes before serving. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream if desired.

Tips

  • For a more decadent dessert, add a pinch of nutmeg to the batter.
  • If using canned peaches, make sure to drain them well to avoid excess liquid in the filling.
  • Top with fresh whipped cream or a scoop of ice cream for extra indulgence!
