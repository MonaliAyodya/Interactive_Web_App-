Mediterranean Diet Bowl

Description

This Mediterranean Diet Bowl is a colorful, nutrient-rich dish packed with fresh veggies, whole grains, healthy fats, and lean protein. It's a perfect representation of the heart-healthy Mediterranean lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas or grilled chicken
  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, add a layer of cooked quinoa or rice as the base.
  2. Arrange the tomatoes, cucumber, onion, olives, and chickpeas (or chicken) on top.
  3. Add crumbled feta and a scoop of hummus in the center.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then mix before serving or enjoy as a composed bowl.

Tips

  • Use whole grains like farro, bulgur, or couscous for variation.
