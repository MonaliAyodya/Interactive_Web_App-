Egg Roll Recipe

Description

This crispy Egg Roll recipe features a flavorful filling of seasoned pork, shredded cabbage, and carrots, all rolled up in a crunchy wrapper. Perfect as an appetizer or party snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 20 egg roll wrappers
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground pork until no longer pink. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add garlic, cabbage, carrots, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, salt, and pepper. Cook until vegetables are soft. Remove from heat and let the filling cool.
  3. Place about 2 tablespoons of filling in the center of each egg roll wrapper. Fold the bottom corner up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll up tightly. Seal the edge with a bit of water.
  4. Heat 2 inches of vegetable oil in a deep skillet to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry egg rolls a few at a time until golden brown and crispy, about 3–4 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve warm with sweet and sour sauce or soy sauce for dipping.

Tips

  • Make sure the filling is cool before rolling to prevent soggy wrappers.
  • Freeze uncooked egg rolls on a baking sheet, then transfer to a freezer bag for future use.
  • You can also bake them at 400°F (200°C) for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through, for a lighter version.
