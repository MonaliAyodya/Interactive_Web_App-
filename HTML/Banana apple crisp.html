Banana Apple Crisp

Description

This Banana Apple Crisp is a delicious, cozy dessert made with sweetened apples, ripe bananas, and a crumbly topping. Perfect for fall or any time you're craving a warm, fruity treat.

Ingredients

  • 3 large apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples and bananas with the lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a pinch of salt. Spread the fruit mixture evenly in the prepared baking dish.
  3. In another bowl, combine the rolled oats, flour, brown sugar, and melted butter. Stir until the mixture forms a crumbly topping.
  4. Sprinkle the oat mixture evenly over the fruit layer in the baking dish.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the fruit is tender.
  6. Allow to cool slightly before serving. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream for an extra treat.

Tips

  • If you prefer a sweeter crisp, increase the amount of brown sugar in the topping.
  • You can use other fruits such as pears or berries along with the apples and bananas for a different flavor.
  • To make the topping extra crunchy, add some chopped nuts like walnuts or pecans to the oat mixture.
