Sheet-Pan Strawberry Shortcake

Description

This Sheet-Pan Strawberry Shortcake is a fun, easy twist on a classic dessert. Soft, fluffy shortcake baked in a sheet pan and topped with juicy strawberries and whipped cream—perfect for feeding a crowd!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 4 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (for strawberries)
  • Whipped cream, for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease or line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry cutter or fingers until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. In another bowl, whisk milk, vanilla, and egg together. Add to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared sheet pan.
  6. Bake for 18–22 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool completely.
  7. Meanwhile, mix strawberries with 2 tablespoons of sugar and let sit for 15–20 minutes to macerate.
  8. Once the shortcake is cool, cut into squares, top with strawberries and whipped cream, and serve.

Tips

  • You can make the shortcake ahead and store it covered at room temperature for up to a day.
  • Use homemade whipped cream for the best flavor.
  • Add a dash of lemon zest to the strawberries for a fresh twist.
