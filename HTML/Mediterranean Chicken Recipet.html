Mediterranean Chicken

Description

This Mediterranean Chicken is juicy, herb-marinated, and packed with classic flavors like garlic, lemon, oregano, and olives. It’s an easy, healthy dish perfect for weeknight dinners or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate the chicken in this mixture for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for deeper flavor).
  3. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook chicken for 5–7 minutes per side until golden and fully cooked through.
  5. Serve topped with olives and a sprinkle of fresh parsley.
  6. Pair with roasted vegetables, couscous, or a Greek salad.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, marinate the chicken overnight in the fridge.
  • You can also bake the chicken at 400°F (200°C) for 25–30 minutes.
