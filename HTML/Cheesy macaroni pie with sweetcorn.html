Cheesy Macaroni Pie with Sweetcorn

Description

This Cheesy Macaroni Pie with Sweetcorn is a comfort food favorite, perfect for family gatherings or a cozy meal. The creamy, cheesy macaroni paired with the sweetness of corn makes it a delicious and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni (or pasta of your choice)
  • 1 cup sweetcorn (canned or fresh)
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (optional for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch baking dish and set aside.
  2. Cook the elbow macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and stir to form a roux. Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste.
  4. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking continuously to prevent lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens into a creamy sauce.
  5. Add the cheddar cheese, parmesan cheese, mustard powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir until the cheese melts and the sauce becomes smooth.
  6. Add the cooked macaroni and sweetcorn to the cheese sauce. Stir to combine, ensuring the pasta is well-coated.
  7. Transfer the macaroni mixture into the prepared baking dish. If using breadcrumbs, sprinkle them evenly over the top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your cheesy macaroni pie with sweetcorn!

Tips

  • If you like a bit of spice, add a pinch of cayenne pepper or chili flakes to the cheese sauce.
  • You can swap the sweetcorn for other vegetables like peas, broccoli, or spinach if desired.
  • For a crispier top, broil the pie for the last 2-3 minutes of baking.
