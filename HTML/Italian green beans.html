Italian Green Beans

Description

Italian Green Beans are a delicious side dish made with tender green beans sautéed in olive oil with garlic, tomatoes, and Italian herbs. They are a flavorful and healthy addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved (or 1/2 cup canned diced tomatoes)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blanch green beans in boiling water for 3–4 minutes until tender-crisp. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  4. Add tomatoes and cook for 2–3 minutes until softened.
  5. Add green beans, oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss well to coat and heat through.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.

Tips

  • Use fresh green beans for the best texture and flavor.
  • Add a splash of balsamic vinegar or lemon juice for brightness.
