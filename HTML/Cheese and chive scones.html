Cheese and Chive Scones

Description

These Cheese and Chive Scones are savory, fluffy, and full of flavor. Perfect as a snack, side dish, or served warm with butter, they make a delightful addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter, diced
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 2/3 cup milk (plus extra for brushing)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the cold butter and rub it into the flour mixture with your fingers until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Stir in the cheese and chives.
  5. Gradually add the milk and mix until a soft dough forms. Do not overmix.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and gently pat it to about 1-inch thickness.
  7. Use a round cutter to cut out scones and place them on the prepared baking tray.
  8. Brush the tops with milk and bake for 15–18 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Cool slightly before serving. Best enjoyed warm with butter.

Tips

  • Use cold butter for the flakiest scones.
  • Experiment with different cheeses like Gruyère or feta for variety.
  • Freeze leftover scones and reheat in the oven for a fresh-baked taste.
