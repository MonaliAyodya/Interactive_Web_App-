Mediterranean Pasta

Description

This Mediterranean Pasta is a deliciously fresh and vibrant dish made with sun-dried tomatoes, olives, feta cheese, and fresh herbs. It's quick to prepare and perfect for a healthy lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250g penne or pasta of choice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/3 cup Kalamata olives, halved
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add sun-dried tomatoes, cherry tomatoes, and olives. Cook for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked pasta and toss everything together. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in crumbled feta.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm or chilled.

Tips

  • Add grilled chicken or chickpeas for extra protein.
  • Drizzle with balsamic glaze for an added Mediterranean twist.
