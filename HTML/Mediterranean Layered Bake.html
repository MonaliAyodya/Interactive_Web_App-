Mediterranean Layered Bake

Description

This Mediterranean Layered Bake is a wholesome dish featuring layers of roasted vegetables, tomato sauce, and creamy cheese. It’s hearty, comforting, and packed with bold Mediterranean flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup marinara or tomato basil sauce
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush vegetables with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes until tender.
  2. In a baking dish, spread a layer of tomato sauce. Add a layer of roasted vegetables, then sprinkle with feta and mozzarella.
  3. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up. Top with Parmesan cheese and dried oregano.
  4. Bake for 20–25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  5. Let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.

Tips

  • Add cooked lentils or chickpeas for extra protein.
  • Serve with a side of crusty bread or a light green salad.
  • Try adding thin potato slices for a heartier version.
