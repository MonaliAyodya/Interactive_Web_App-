Lentil Soup

Description

This hearty and comforting Lentil Soup is packed with nutritious lentils, vegetables, and savory spices. It’s a filling, healthy, and budget-friendly meal that is perfect for any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cups fresh spinach (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté for 3-4 minutes, until the onion becomes translucent.
  2. Add the carrots and celery to the pot and sauté for another 5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
  3. Stir in the lentils, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, turmeric, coriander, black pepper, salt, and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender and the soup has thickened.
  5. Once the soup is ready, remove the bay leaves and stir in the spinach, if using. Cook for another 2 minutes until the spinach wilts.
  6. For extra flavor, add a splash of lemon juice before serving.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy with a slice of crusty bread or a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Tips

  • If you prefer a smoother texture, blend part of the soup with an immersion blender or regular blender.
  • You can freeze leftover soup for up to 3 months. Just be sure to cool it completely before freezing.
  • For extra protein, add cooked chicken or sausage to the soup.
