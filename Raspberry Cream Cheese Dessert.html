Raspberry Cream Cheese Dessert

Description

This Raspberry Cream Cheese Dessert is a smooth and creamy no-bake delight with layers of tangy raspberry topping and rich cream cheese over a buttery graham cracker crust. It’s a perfect treat for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups fresh raspberries (or frozen, thawed)
  • 1/4 cup raspberry jam or preserves
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press firmly into a 9x9-inch baking dish. Chill for 15–20 minutes to set.
  2. In another bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract; beat until combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the chilled crust.
  5. In a small saucepan, warm the raspberry jam until slightly runny. Stir in the fresh raspberries gently and remove from heat.
  6. Spread the raspberry topping over the cream cheese layer.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 3–4 hours before serving.
  8. Slice into squares and garnish with mint leaves if desired.

Tips

  • Make sure cream cheese is fully softened to avoid lumps.
  • You can swap raspberries for strawberries or blueberries if preferred.
  • For a firmer crust, bake it at 350°F (175°C) for 8 minutes before chilling.
