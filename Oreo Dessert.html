Oreo Dessert

Description

This no-bake Oreo dessert is a delicious treat with a creamy, rich filling and a crunchy cookie crust. Itâ€™s the perfect easy dessert for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 24 Oreo cookies (crushed)
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Additional crushed Oreos for topping

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the crushed Oreos and melted butter. Mix until the crumbs are coated. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch pie dish or baking dish to form a crust. Refrigerate while preparing the filling.
  2. In a separate large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and powdered sugar together until smooth and creamy.
  3. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract together until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture until fully combined.
  5. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the prepared Oreo crust. Use a spatula to smooth the top.
  6. Crush additional Oreos and sprinkle them on top of the dessert for garnish.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the dessert to set. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Tips

  • You can add chocolate syrup or hot fudge drizzle for extra flavor.
  • Use mini Oreos for a different twist on the classic cookie crust.
  • For an extra creamy filling, add a little more heavy cream to the mixture.
