Ice Cream Sandwich Desserts

Description

This fun and easy dessert takes the classic ice cream sandwich to the next level. With layers of your favorite ice cream, cookies, and toppings, it's a perfect summer treat or party dessert!

Ingredients

  • 12 large cookies (chocolate chip, vanilla, or your choice)
  • 1 quart vanilla ice cream (or any flavor you like)
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips (or other toppings like sprinkles, crushed candies, or nuts)
  • 1/4 cup caramel sauce (optional)
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place a layer of plastic wrap or parchment paper on a baking sheet for easy cleanup.
  2. Lay out 6 cookies on the sheet, face up. Scoop a generous amount of ice cream and place it on top of each cookie. Use a spoon or ice cream scooper to spread it evenly.
  3. Top each with another cookie, pressing down lightly to sandwich the ice cream between them.
  4. Roll the edges of the ice cream sandwich in mini chocolate chips or your preferred toppings to coat the sides.
  5. For an extra indulgent treat, drizzle with caramel sauce and add a dollop of whipped cream on top.
  6. Wrap each sandwich in plastic wrap and freeze for at least 2 hours to firm up the ice cream.
  7. Once frozen, slice into smaller portions if desired and serve cold. Enjoy!

Tips

  • For a healthier version, use lower-fat ice cream and cookies with less sugar.
  • Try using different cookies such as oatmeal, gingerbread, or peanut butter for variety.
  • If you want to make these ahead of time, store them in an airtight container in the freezer for up to a week.
