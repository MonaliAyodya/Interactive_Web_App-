Butterscotch Pecan Dessert

Description

This rich and indulgent butterscotch pecan dessert features a creamy butterscotch filling, topped with toasted pecans, and is perfect for special occasions or as a decadent treat anytime.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup butterscotch chips
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup chopped pecans (toasted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish or a similar-sized dish.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and graham cracker crumbs until the mixture is well combined. Press it into the bottom of the prepared dish to form the crust.
  3. Bake the crust for 8-10 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.
  4. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the butterscotch chips and heavy cream. Stir constantly until the butterscotch chips have melted and the mixture is smooth.
  5. Pour the butterscotch mixture over the cooled crust, spreading it evenly with a spatula.
  6. Sprinkle the chopped toasted pecans over the top, pressing them gently into the filling.
  7. Refrigerate the dessert for at least 2 hours, or until it is set and chilled through.
  8. Once chilled, cut into squares or bars and serve. Optionally, drizzle with more butterscotch sauce before serving.

Tips

  • For a more decadent dessert, top with whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • You can use walnuts or almonds if you prefer different nuts instead of pecans.
  • To make it even more flavorful, try adding a touch of cinnamon or nutmeg to the crust mixture.
