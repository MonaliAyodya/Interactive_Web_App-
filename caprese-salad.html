Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese Salad is a simple and refreshing Italian appetizer made with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, fragrant basil, olive oil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella on a plate.
  2. Place a fresh basil leaf between each slice.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic glaze (if using) over the top.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately as a starter or side dish.

Tips

  • Use the freshest ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Chill the salad slightly before serving on a warm day.
