Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado Toast is a quick, healthy, and delicious meal that’s perfect for breakfast, lunch, or even a snack. Creamy avocado on crisp, toasted bread topped with your favorite extras—it’s endlessly customizable and always satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole-grain or sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon or lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, poached egg, feta cheese, microgreens, olive oil

Instructions

  1. Toast the slices of bread until golden and crispy.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork. Stir in lemon or lime juice, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.
  5. Add your favorite toppings such as sliced cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, a poached egg, or a drizzle of olive oil.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips

  • Use ripe avocados—they should be slightly soft when gently pressed.
  • Add a sprinkle of chili flakes or hot sauce if you like it spicy.
  • Top with a fried or poached egg to make it a complete meal.
  • Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the toast just before serving for extra freshness.
