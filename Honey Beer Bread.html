Honey Beer Bread

Description

This Honey Beer Bread is a quick and easy no-yeast bread that’s hearty, slightly sweet, and full of flavor. Perfect with soups, stews, or enjoyed with a pat of butter right out of the oven.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 (12 oz) bottle of beer (any variety)
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add honey and beer, and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and drizzle melted butter over the top.
  5. Bake for 45–55 minutes, or until the top is golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Tips

  • Different beers will subtly affect the flavor—try a lager for mild taste or a stout for a richer loaf.
  • For extra texture, mix in shredded cheese or herbs before baking.
  • This bread toasts well and makes great sandwiches too!
