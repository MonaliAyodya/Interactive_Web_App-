Mediterranean Nachos

Description

These Mediterranean Nachos are a fresh and healthy twist on the classic nachos. Crispy tortilla chips are topped with seasoned ground lamb or beef, crumbled feta, Kalamata olives, cucumbers, tomatoes, and a drizzle of tzatziki sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tortilla chips
  • 1/2 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tzatziki sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Arrange the tortilla chips in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground lamb or beef over medium heat until browned, about 5–7 minutes. Add cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and cook for another 2 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Spread the cooked meat over the tortilla chips. Sprinkle crumbled feta, olives, cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onion on top.
  4. Bake in the oven for 5–7 minutes, or until the chips are crispy and the cheese is slightly melted.
  5. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley. Drizzle with tzatziki sauce before serving.

Tips

  • For a spicier kick, add some chopped jalapeños or drizzle with hot sauce.
  • Use Greek yogurt instead of tzatziki for a lighter version.
  • Make sure to serve immediately while the nachos are still crispy.
