Chocolate Cake

Description

This moist and decadent chocolate cake is made with simple ingredients and topped with a rich, velvety chocolate ganache. It's the perfect dessert for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. In another bowl, mix together the sugars, eggs, vanilla, buttermilk, and oil until smooth.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined.
  5. Add the hot water to the batter and stir until smooth (the batter will be thin).
  6. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from the pans and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Chocolate Ganache

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate, chopped

Ganache Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  2. Remove from heat and pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate in a bowl.
  3. Let it sit for 5 minutes, then whisk until smooth and glossy.
  4. Pour the ganache over the cooled cake and let it drip down the sides. Allow the ganache to set before serving.

Tips

  • For an extra touch, sprinkle some chocolate shavings or fresh berries on top of the ganache.
  • Use full-fat sour cream instead of buttermilk for a richer flavor.
