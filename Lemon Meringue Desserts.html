Lemon Meringue Dessert

Description

This Lemon Meringue Dessert features a zesty lemon filling topped with a light, fluffy meringue on a buttery crust. It’s a refreshing and tangy treat, perfect for spring and summer occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar (for filling)
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons sugar (for meringue)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press into a 9-inch pie pan and bake for 8–10 minutes. Let cool.
  3. In a saucepan, whisk 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt. Stir in water, lemon juice, and zest. Cook over medium heat, stirring until thickened. Stir in butter.
  4. In a small bowl, gradually whisk hot mixture into egg yolks, then return to the saucepan and bring to a gentle boil. Remove from heat and pour into prepared crust.
  5. In a clean bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually add 6 tablespoons sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
  6. Spread meringue over lemon filling, sealing the edges.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
  8. Let cool completely before slicing. Chill in the fridge for best results.

Tips

  • Make sure no yolk gets into the egg whites to help the meringue whip properly.
  • Use fresh lemons for the best flavor and aroma.
  • To prevent meringue from weeping, spread it while the lemon filling is still hot.
