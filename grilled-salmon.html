Grilled Salmon

Description

Grilled Salmon is a healthy, flavorful main course featuring tender, flaky salmon fillets marinated with herbs and lemon, then cooked to perfection on a grill or grill pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (skin-on or skinless)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried dill or fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, dill, salt, and pepper.
  2. Brush the mixture over both sides of the salmon fillets. Let marinate for 15–30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill or grill pan over medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  4. Place salmon fillets skin-side down on the grill. Cook for 4–5 minutes per side, depending on thickness.
  5. Remove from grill once the salmon is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges and your choice of sides.

Tips

  • For best results, don’t overcook the salmon. It should remain moist and tender inside.
  • Pair with grilled vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete meal.
