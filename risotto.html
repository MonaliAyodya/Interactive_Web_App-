Risotto

Description

Risotto is a creamy Italian rice dish cooked slowly in broth, often enriched with butter, cheese, vegetables, or seafood.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, warmed
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© the onion until soft.
  2. Add Arborio rice and stir for 1-2 minutes until lightly toasted.
  3. Pour in wine and stir until absorbed (skip if not using wine).
  4. Add warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and letting it absorb before adding more.
  5. Continue for 18-20 minutes until rice is creamy and al dente.
  6. Stir in Parmesan, season with salt and pepper, and serve warm.

Tips

  • Stirring constantly helps release the starch for that creamy texture.
  • Try adding mushrooms, peas, or shrimp for variety.
